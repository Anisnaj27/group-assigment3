M SPICE HOTEL

Ernes Hotel is a unique 3-star conference and apartment-style hotel located right in the city center of Sungai Petani and it was re-built in 2018. The hotel is celebrated across the country for being Malaysia's very first hospitality venture transformed and up-cycled from an old 1960 Police Barracks building. It was once housed local law enforcement decades ago has been completely reimagined into a modern, vibrant hospitality, accommodation and large-scale corporate event hub.

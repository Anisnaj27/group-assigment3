THE BLANKET HOTEL

The Blanket Hotel is a three-star boutique hotel located in Seberang Jaya, Penang. Situated near Sunway Carnival Mall, restaurants, banks, and commercial outlets, the hotel is particularly popular among business travellers and guests seeking affordable accommodation with convenient access to nearby amenities. The hotel emphasizes simplicity, comfort, and practicality through its modern minimalist design.

HOTEL EXTERIOR & GROUNDS

The hotel occupies a converted commercial building within the Seberang Jaya business district. Its exterior presents a clean and contemporary appearance with easy access to surrounding facilities. While the property does not feature landscaped gardens or resort-style grounds, its urban setting provides convenience for guests travelling for work or short stays.

RECEPTION & LOBBY

The reception area adopts a simple and modern concept with a shared lounge space near the entrance. The front desk operates 24 hours a day, allowing flexibility for guests arriving at different times. Reviews frequently highlight the helpfulness and friendliness of the staff, contributing positively to the overall guest experience.

ACCOMMODATION

The Blanket Hotel offers a variety of room types designed to accommodate different categories of travellers, including solo guests, couples, business travellers, and families. The available accommodations include Superior Queen, Superior Twin, Queen Deluxe, Twin Deluxe, King Deluxe, and Family Rooms.

The Blanket Hotel provides clean, comfortable, and functional accommodation that caters to both short-term and extended stays while offering good value for money.

ROOM REVIEW

Each room is designed with a modern and minimalist concept, emphasizing comfort and practicality. Standard room amenities include air conditioning, complimentary Wi-Fi, a flat-screen television, tea and coffee-making facilities, bottled water, a work desk, an in-room safe, and a private bathroom equipped with essential toiletries. Larger room options, such as the King Deluxe and Family Rooms, provide additional space and are suitable for families or small groups.

AMANJAYA HOTEL

Amanjaya Hotel is a budget-friendly hotel located in Bandar Amanjaya, Sungai Petani, Kedah. The hotel is known for offering comfortable accommodation with essential amenities at an affordable price. Its strategic location near commercial areas, restaurants, and retail outlets makes it a convenient choice for both business and leisure travellers. The hotel has received positive feedback for its cleanliness, friendly staff, and accessibility, making it a popular option among visitors seeking a practical and comfortable stay.

FIRST IMPRESSION

Amanjaya Hotel presents a modern and well-maintained exterior that blends well with the surrounding commercial district. Although the property is relatively modest in size, its clean appearance and organized layout create a welcoming atmosphere for guests. The hotel's location within Bandar Amanjaya also contributes to its appeal, as various shops, eateries, and services are located nearby.

ACCOMMODATION

Amanjaya Hotel offers a range of accommodation options suitable for solo travellers, couples, families, and business guests. The hotel provides several room categories, including Standard Rooms, Deluxe Rooms, Superior Rooms, and Family Rooms, each designed to provide comfort and convenience at an affordable price.

The hotel focuses on providing clean, comfortable, and practical accommodation, ensuring that guests have a pleasant stay whether they are visiting Sungai Petani for business or leisure purposes. Its combination of essential facilities and affordable rates makes Amanjaya Hotel a popular choice among budget-conscious travellers.

ROOM REVIEW

The rooms feature a modern and functional design with essential amenities such as air conditioning, complimentary Wi-Fi, a flat-screen television, a work desk, tea and coffee-making facilities, bottled water, and private bathrooms with basic toiletries. Family rooms offer larger living spaces to accommodate multiple guests comfortably, making them suitable for families or small groups.

ZAQUES HOTEL

Zaques Hotel is a unique 3-star conference and apartment-style hotel located right in the city center of Sungai Petani and it was re-built in 2018. The hotel is celebrated across the country for being Malaysia's very first hospitality venture transformed and up-cycled from an old 1960 Police Barracks building. It was once housed local law enforcement decades ago has been completely reimagined into a modern, vibrant hospitality, accommodation and large-scale corporate event hub.

HOTEL EXTERIOR & GROUNDS

The exterior and grounds of Zaques Hotel preserve a historic, post-colonial silhouette that reflects the strict architectural lines of 1960 Malaysian police barracks. This heritage structure features well-manicured gardens illuminated by warm bistro string lights, creating a photogenic evening setting with clear views of local Kedah sunsets. A massive, free on-site private parking lot easily accommodates large vehicle convoys, making it a popular regional hub for car meets and tour buses. Blending this history with local village charm, the open grounds also host a quirks mini livestock enclosure with friendly roaming goats. offering a uniques family friendly countryside vibe right in the middle of town.

RECEPTION & LOBBY

The ground floor lobby features a breezy, open-concept layout with plush seating specifically designed to manage heavy foot traffic from tour groups and wedding parties. Operating around the clock, the 24-hour front desk is run by local, bilingual staff known for their warm northern Malaysian hospitality. Additionally, the space is strategically built with integrated event entryways that serve as secondary counters, keeping seminar and wedding registrations completely separate from the main hotel check-in desk.

ACCOMMODATION

The hotel features a large inventory of 74 rooms and apartments that can comfortably accommodate up to 252 guests simultaneously. The selection includes premium VIP Suites with lounge seating for couples or executives, and comfortable Deluxe Queen and Twin rooms tailored for weekend travelers and corporate guests. A rare standout feature for the property is its multi-level apartment units across levels one to four, which come fully furnished with dedicated family living rooms and multiple bathrooms for large staycations.

ROOM REVIEW

Independent guest feedback consistently praises the exceptionally large, airy room dimensions, noting that even standard spaces offer far more square footage than modern boutique hotels. Visitors also highly rate the powerful climate control, as individual air conditioning units quickly cool the rooms to provide immediate relief from the tropical heat. The interior aesthetic leans into a charming, nostalgic retro look, utilizing simple wooden furniture and vintage parquet flooring to preserve the property's historic roots.

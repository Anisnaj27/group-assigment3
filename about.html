ABOUT US

INTRODUCTION

Welcome to our 10 Hotel in Kedah & Penang Review Website, a project developed as part of the IML470 course. This website aims to provide comprehensive reviews and comparisons of ten selected hotels. This will help users make informed decisions when choosing accommodation. The project combines information management, research and web development skills to create an informative and user-friendly platforms for travelers.

OUR JOURNEY

Our journey in finding and reviewing the ten selected hotels was both educational and rewarding. We conducted research using hotel websites, travel platforms such as agoda, and customer reviews to gather accurate information about different types of hotels, and budget accommodations. By comparing factors such as facilities, services, location, pricing, and guest feedback, we were able to create fair and informative reviews. Throughout the project, we worked together to organize and analyze the information, which helped us improve our research, teamwork, and web development skills. This experience gave us a better understanding of the hospitality industry and the importance of providing reliable information to help users make informed hotel choices.

OBJECTIVES

  1. To review and compare ten selected hotels.
  2. To provide reliable and useful information for travelers.
  3. To evaluate hotels based on facilities, services, and customer satisfaction.
  4. To develop a user-friendly website for accessing hotel reviews.
  5. To apply information management and web development skills learned throughout the course.

MISSION

Our mission is to provide accurate, reliable, and well-organized hotel reviews that assist travelers in making informed accommodation decisions. We aim to present information in a clear and accessible manner while promoting transparency and fairness in hotel evaluations.

VISION

Our vision is to become a trusted platform that helps users compare hotels effectively and enhances their travel planning experience. We aspire to provide high-quality information that contributes to better decision-making and greater customer satisfaction.

TARGET AUDIENCES

  • Domestic and international travelers.
  • Tourists planning vacations.
  • Business travelers.
  • Students in budget.
  • Individuals seeking hotel.

IMPORTANCE OF HOTEL REVIEW

Hotel reviews play a crucial role in helping customers understand the strengths and weaknesses of a hotel before making reservations. Reviews provide valuable insights into real guest experiences and allow travelers to compare accommodation options more effectively. Furthermore, reviews encourage hotels to maintain high service standards and continuously improve customer satisfaction.

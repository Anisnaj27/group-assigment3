S Hotel Seberang Jaya

S Hotel Seberang Jaya is a budget-friendly 2-star hotel located in the heart of Seberang Jaya, Penang. The hotel is strategically situated near Sunway Carnival Mall, Sunway Medical Centre, Penang Bird Park, and the Perai Industrial Area, making it a convenient choice for both business and leisure travellers.

RECEPTION & LOBBY

The reception area at S Hotel Seberang Jaya features a 24-hour front desk that provides convenient check-in and guest services. The lobby has a simple and modern design with comfortable seating, creating a welcoming and comfortable atmosphere for guests.

ACCOMMODATION

S Hotel offers a variety of room types, including Standard Rooms, Deluxe Rooms, Family Rooms, and Suites. The rooms are equipped with air conditioning, free Wi-Fi, flat-screen televisions, private bathrooms, work desks, electric kettles, and complimentary toiletries to provide a comfortable stay.

ROOM REVIEW

Each room is designed with a modern and minimalist concept, emphasizing comfort and practicality. Standard room amenities include air conditioning, complimentary Wi-Fi, a flat-screen television, tea and coffee-making facilities, bottled water, a work desk, an in-room safe, and a private bathroom equipped with essential toiletries. Larger room options, such as the King Deluxe and Family Rooms, provide additional space and are suitable for families or small groups.

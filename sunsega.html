Sunsega Hotel

Sunsega Hotel is a budget hotel located in Perai, Penang. It is designed to provide affordable accommodation for travellers, workers, and visitors to the industrial and commercial areas surrounding Perai and Seberang Jaya. The hotel offers practical facilities and easy access to major roads, shopping centres, and industrial zones.

RECEPTION & LOBBY

The reception area at Sunsega Hotel features a 24-hour front desk that provides convenient check-in, check-out, and guest assistance. The lobby has a simple and modern design with a comfortable seating area, creating a clean and welcoming atmosphere for both business and leisure travellers.

ACCOMMODATION

The hotel offers standard guest rooms equipped with basic amenities to meet the needs of short-stay travellers. Rooms generally include air conditioning, private bathrooms, television, Wi-Fi access, and essential furnishings for a comfortable stay.

ROOM REVIEW

The rooms at Sunsega Hotel are simple, clean, and designed to provide a comfortable stay for both business and leisure travellers. Each room is equipped with basic amenities such as air conditioning, free Wi-Fi, a flat-screen television, and a private bathroom. The rooms offer a practical and cosy environment, making them suitable for short stays and guests looking for affordable accommodation in Perai.

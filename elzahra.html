EL ZAHRA HOTEL

Hotel El-Zahraa is a budget-friendly hotel located in the Lagenda Heights area of Sungai Petani. The hotel is known for its comfortable accommodation, affordable rates, and unique interior design inspired by Arabic and Middle Eastern elements. It offers convenient access to nearby shops, restaurants, banks, and commercial facilities, making it a suitable choice for both business and leisure travelers

HOTEL LOBBY

The hotel lobby has a very cozy and welcoming look. It features comfortable grey velvet sofas arranged around a matching glass-top coffee table. In the background, there is a prominent sign for "Hotel El-Zahraa" placed above a decorative console table with a bright blue arch design. The space is also decorated with festive hanging ornaments and plants, making it feel like a very friendly and pleasant place for guests to sit and relax.

RECEPTION

The hotel reception area looks grand and very beautiful. The front desk stands out with its lovely patterns, placed between tall marble pillars under bright white arches. A large, sparkling chandelier hangs from a unique star-shaped ceiling design, which matches the beautiful geometric pattern on the floor below. With its long grey velvet sofa, elegant staircase, and bright lighting, this area feels very upscale and comfortable for guests when they walk in.

INTERIOR DESIGN

The interior design of this area is beautiful and grand. It combines elegant white arches and smooth marble pillars with traditional, detailed patterns on the front desk and the floor. A striking curved staircase with pretty metal railings sits on the right side, while cozy velvet seating and a unique blue surfboard wall feature add a pleasant touch to the left. The bright lighting and mix of fine materials make this a very attractive and comfortable space for arriving guests.

ROOM REVIEW

This is a spacious and well-equipped hotel room designed for a very comfortable stay. The main area features a neatly made large bed with rolled towels ready for guests, alongside a separate armchair for extra seating. For entertainment, a television is set up on a long wooden bench directly across from the resting area. The room includes a large arched window that lets in unique colored light, as well as expansive floor-to-ceiling curtains that can be closed completely to block out the light for a peaceful night's sleep.

HOTEL LOCATION

Hotel El-Zahraa is in an excellent location just 1.04 kilometers from the Sungai Petani city center, earning a fantastic 8.9 location rating score. It is a paradise for shoppers, sitting just a short 260-meter walk from Village Mall, 390 meters from Tesco Sungai Petani (Utara), and only 2.0 kilometers from Amanjaya Mall. Families will love how close it is to The Carnival Theme Park, which is only 1.7 kilometers away. Important local landmarks are also just a quick drive away, including the Sungai Petani Clock Tower at 2.3 kilometers and Masjid Sultan Muzaffar Shah at 2.4 kilometers.
